Project AEDES

Advanced Early Detection and Exploration Service

AEDES aims to improve public health response against dengue in the Philippines by predicting dengue cases from climate and digital data and pinpointing possible hotspots from satellite data. Using AEDES will help get ahead of the lagged delay of dengue reporting by using real-time information to infer if dengue cases and deaths are about to spike and anticipate areas that may be affected by dengue to prioritize health aid, supplies, and proactive fumigation to prevent the outbreaks.
